A Poem for my Playlist

The description for one of my playlists from when I was being a little too sappy.

June 25, 2024.

Something to keep for my heart.

It's all in an effort to feel something, be something.

And because of it, we always need something, want something.

Maybe it's not worth it to try...but it's all I've ever known.

Whispers, tears, songs and cries.

Wishes, prayers, and heartbeats.
